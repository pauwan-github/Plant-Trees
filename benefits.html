Benefits of trees include:

  1. Trees increase property value.
  2. Clean air.
  3. Trees slow water runoff.
  4. Trees prevent soil erosion.
  5. Trees buffer noise
  6. Trees cool our homes.
  7. Trees can save you money on energy cost.
  8. Trees are beautiful
  9. Trees are Sacred.
  10. Most Medicines Are Derived from trees.

TREES INCREASE PROPERTY VALUES

Everyone wants their home to have the highest possible property value, right? Well landscaping with trees increase your property value as much as 20%, according to estimates.
That is a great return on investment interms of the small amount of upkeep they require.

CLEAN AIR

Trees can absorb as much as 48 pounds of carbon(iv)oxide each year and can sequester 1 ton of carbon(iv)oxide by the time it reaches 40 years old.
In addition, trees absorb odors and pollutant gases such as nitrogen oxides, ammonia, sulfur and ozone and filter particulates out of the air by trapping them on their leaves and bark.

TREES SLOW WATER RUNOFF

Flash flooding can be significantly reduced by planting trees.
According to About.com, a Colorado Blue Spruce, either planted or growing wild, can intercept more than 1000 gallons of water anually when fully grown, which helps recharge underground aquifers.

TREES PREVENT SOIL EROSION

A tree's root system binds the soil and its leaves provide a barrier to the wind and rain; thus preventing soil erosion.

TREES BUFFER NOISE

Trees are planted at strategic points in a neighbourhood or around your house can abate major noises. This is especially nice if you live near a freeway or airport.

TREES COOL OUR HOMES

Trees can cool a city by up to 10 degreed Fahrenheitby shading homes and streets, breaking up urban "heat islands" and releasing water vapour into the air through their leaves.

TREES CAN SAVE YOU MONEY ON ENERGY COSTS

Building off the previous point, strategically placed trees can save homeowners up to 25% on energy bills by providing shade in the summer and windbreaks in the winter.

TREES ARE BEAUTIFUL

This is a no-brainer. Trees are stunning parts of our natural landscape. From the first buds of spring th the colourful leaves in the fall, trees are gorgeous. They make great decoration indoors and outdoors.

TREES ARE SACRED

Trees were sacred to many ancient worlds and still hold special meaning in many cultures today. The Druids believed a number of trees to be sacred, including the Ash Tree from which healing wands were made. Today, many people in Madagascar worship them because they believe their ancestor's spirits live in the forests.

MOST MEDICINES ARE DERIVED FROM TREES

Did you know that aspirin was derived from willow bark? Or that dogwood bark has been used as a substitute for quinine? While many drugs that come from plants and trees have been replaced by synthetics, there is no denying how important they are when it comes to modern medicine and research.
