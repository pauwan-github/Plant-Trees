Tree Planting Process

Tree planting process is the activity in which a tree is put in the soil to grow. It is a special activity by which should be taken seriously by all.

Benefits of trees include:

  1. Survey.
  2. Dig Hole.
  3. Sprinkle Water.

SURVEY THE AREA OF TREE PLANTING

Survey the area that you want to plant the tree. You can use the followwing techniques.
  1. It should be in an open field. (The area should not be covered from sunlight).
  2. The are should be animal free. (Animals shouuld not trespass the area)
  3. The area should be free from human activities such as: walking, playing, parking cars, dumping litters, gathering, etc.
  4. The area should not be so close to a factory, as the factories could pollute the air for the trees.

DIG A HOLE OF AN APPROPRIATE SIZE

Dig a hole that can appropriately fit the size of the tree to be planted. You can do this by use of a shovel, a jembe among others.

SPRINKLE WATER

Gently sprinkle water around and inside the hole. make sure that the place is well moistened not flooded.
